SHANE'S NEW JOB by Ran
https://www.nexusmods.com/stardewvalley/mods/2894


Pull Shane out of unemployment after JojaMart shuts down.
He will now work at the saloon.


FEATURES:
- Changes Shane’s schedule post-Joja: After seeing the community center event and saying bye to JojaMart, he will start working at the saloon instead of staying home at Marnie’s
- New work animations
- Removes sad jobless dialogue

This only works when Shane is unmarried and Joja is gone.


REQUIREMENTS:
- SMAPI
- Content Patcher


INSTALL:
- Install the latest version of SMAPI and Content Patcher
- Download and unzip files
- Place folder in “\Stardew Valley\Mods”
- Run game with SMAPI
